About WealthDelay
Free financial clarity tools — no login, no data stored

WealthDelay exists to answer one question: what does every financial decision truly cost you — not in dollars today, but in lifetime wealth?

The Problem We Solve

Most people know their coffee costs $5. Very few know it costs $187,000 over a career. Not because they're bad with money — because nobody shows them the compounding math in real terms.

Banks profit when you carry balances. Subscription services profit from autopay inertia. The entire consumer economy is designed to make spending feel smaller than it is. WealthDelay is the counterweight: instant, honest, compounded truth.

How It Works

Every WealthDelay tool runs entirely in your browser using verified financial formulas — the same math used by certified financial planners. No data is sent to any server. No account required. No paywall.

  • Opportunity cost — what money could have grown to if invested instead
  • Real (inflation-adjusted) values — what future dollars are worth today
  • Life-hours — the irreplaceable time traded for spending decisions
  • Retirement impact — months of freedom gained or lost
