Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2025

Short version: WealthDelay collects no personal data. All calculations run entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. No accounts, no cookies, no tracking of your inputs.

What We Collect

WealthDelay does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The financial inputs you enter into our calculators (age, salary, spending amounts, etc.) never leave your device. They exist only in your browser's memory and are discarded when you close the tab.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand aggregate site usage — things like which pages are visited, how long people stay, and what devices are used. This data is anonymous and aggregated. Google Analytics does not have access to your calculator inputs. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Advertising

WealthDelay displays Google AdSense advertisements to support free access to our tools. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. We may also display affiliate links to financial products. These are clearly marked. We may earn a commission if you sign up through these links, at no cost to you.

Cookies

WealthDelay itself does not set any cookies. Google Analytics and Google AdSense may set cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

Our tools may contain links to third-party websites (brokerages, banks, financial tools). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any site you visit.

Children's Privacy

WealthDelay is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of WealthDelay after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at privacy@wealthdelay.com.
