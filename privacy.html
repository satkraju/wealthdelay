Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026

Short version: WealthDelay collects no personal financial data. Every number you enter into our calculators stays in your browser and is never transmitted to any server. We use Google Analytics for anonymous traffic data and Google AdSense for advertising.

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how WealthDelay ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use our website at wealthdelay.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about what we collect, what we do not collect, and how we use the information we do receive.

WealthDelay is a free financial calculator tool. Our core commitment is that the financial inputs you enter — your salary, spending amounts, debt balances, savings rates, and any other personal financial information — are never collected, stored, or transmitted. This is a technical guarantee, not just a policy promise. Our calculators are built so that all computation happens locally in your browser with no server communication.

Information We Do Not Collect

The following categories of information are never collected by WealthDelay under any circumstances:

  • Financial inputs you enter into any calculator (salary, spending, debt amounts, savings, etc.)
  • Your name, address, phone number, or any other personally identifying information
  • Your IP address (beyond what Google Analytics processes anonymously)
  • Calculator results or outputs generated for you
  • Any information about your financial situation, goals, or history

This means WealthDelay cannot and does not build a profile of your financial situation. We have no way to identify you individually from your use of our calculators.

Information We Do Collect — Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how people use WealthDelay at an aggregate level. Google Analytics collects anonymous, aggregated data including:

  • Which pages are visited and how long visitors spend on them
  • General geographic location (country and region level only)
  • Device type, operating system, and browser
  • How visitors arrived at WealthDelay (search engine, direct, referral)
  • Which tools are used most frequently

This data helps us understand which tools are most useful and how to improve the site. Google Analytics does not have access to your calculator inputs. The data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you as an individual.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. You can also use browser privacy features or extensions that block tracking scripts.

Advertising — Google AdSense

WealthDelay displays advertisements through Google AdSense to support the free availability of our tools. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your interests and prior web browsing activity.

Specifically, Google may use information about your visits to this and other websites to show you relevant advertisements. This is governed by Google's own privacy policies and advertising practices, which are separate from WealthDelay's practices.

You have several options to manage advertising preferences:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ad Settings
  • Use the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out tool
  • Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser settings
  • Use a browser extension that blocks advertising trackers

WealthDelay does not share any data about you with Google AdSense beyond what Google's own scripts collect through standard browser interactions.

Affiliate Links

Some pages on WealthDelay contain affiliate links to financial products and services, such as investment platforms and savings accounts. These links are always clearly marked. When you click an affiliate link and sign up for a service, WealthDelay may receive a commission from the partner company at no additional cost to you.

Clicking an affiliate link does not give WealthDelay access to any information about you. The partner company's own privacy policy governs any information you provide when signing up for their service.

We only include affiliate links to products we believe are genuinely useful to our audience. Affiliate relationships never influence our calculator results or the financial information we present.

Payment Processing — Stripe

If you purchase a paid PDF report on WealthDelay, payment is processed by Stripe, Inc. WealthDelay does not collect or store your credit card information. All payment data is handled directly by Stripe in accordance with their privacy policy and PCI DSS compliance standards. You can review Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.

When you make a purchase, Stripe will have access to your email address and payment information as necessary to process the transaction. WealthDelay receives confirmation that a payment was successful but does not receive your full card details.

Cookies

WealthDelay itself does not set any first-party cookies. However, third-party services we use — Google Analytics and Google AdSense — may set cookies in your browser in accordance with their own privacy policies. These cookies are used for analytics and advertising purposes as described above.

You can manage, restrict, or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the display of advertisements on our site, but will not affect the functionality of our calculators in any way.

Third-Party Links

WealthDelay contains links to third-party websites including financial institutions, investment platforms, and other tools. These links are provided for your convenience. WealthDelay is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of any third-party website. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any external site you visit before providing them with personal information.

Children's Privacy

WealthDelay is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through our site, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to address the situation.

Your Rights

Because WealthDelay does not collect personal data tied to your identity, there is generally no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. However, if you have made a purchase through our site or have otherwise provided personal information, you have the right to:

  • Request information about what data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@wealthdelay.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of WealthDelay after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or WealthDelay's privacy practices, please contact us at hello@wealthdelay.com. We take privacy inquiries seriously and will respond promptly.
